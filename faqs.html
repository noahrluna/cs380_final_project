Common Questions About the Wesbite

What is the Cadet Postal Service?

The Cadet Postal Service is a website that allows you to receive updates about when you have a slip or key in your PO Box.

How do I sign up?

To sign up, click on the "Sign Up" button on the home page. You will be asked to enter your name, email, password, and address. Once you have entered your information, click the "Sign Up" button.

How soon will my mail be ready?

Your mail will be ready within 24 hours following a notification. The Cadet Postal Service operates from 8:30am - 3:30 pm Monday through Saturday. Post Office Information
